The following visualization compares traffic sensor data on the Bay Bridge the week of the July BART strike with a visual average of the 2 weeks before and after the strike.

hours
The map shows where each sensor is located. Sliding the yellow bar will calculate average flow and occupancy during the highlighted time period of the strike, and calculate an average for the other weeks.
This project was made with help from Lewis and Jean.
It is part of a larger effort to understand the BART labor dispute in more detail.
